The Best Cities I've Been To

Saigon

This is my favorite city because of how big it is, how many things are there to do and because my family currently lives there.

New York

This is the best city in the USA. The size, the bustling feeling and the vibe is just unique and unmatchable.

Bangkok

Perfect middle ground between Saigon and NYC. Bangkok is big and bustling, while its people are sweet and heartwarming.
